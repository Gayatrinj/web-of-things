Problem Definition

Our survey identifies key challenges in integrating IoT devices into the web, focusing on issues of scalability, security, and real-time data management.

Solution Approach

We propose scalable middleware frameworks and security mechanisms, such as fog and edge computing, to address the growing demands of the Web of Things.

Methodology

Our research methodology includes a systematic review of existing literature, theoretical analysis, and simulation studies to evaluate the performance of Web of Things architectures.

Survey Findings

Our findings suggest that hybrid edge-cloud architectures are essential for scalability and real-time data processing in the Web of Things. Interactive graphs and charts will be added here for better understanding.
